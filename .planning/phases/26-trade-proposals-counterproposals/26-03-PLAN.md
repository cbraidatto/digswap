    3. "Counter" (secondary): Link href={`/trades/new/${counterpartyId}?tradeId=${tradeId}`} (navigates to side-by-side with tradeId pre-filled for counterproposal flow)

    Layout: flex gap-2 with buttons. Error shown below as text-destructive text-sm.
    All buttons disabled when isLoading = true.
