## Decisions Made

- **Deleted dead queries vs. underscore-prefix in perfil/page.tsx:** The three `Promise.all` entries (weeklyAdds, tradesThisWeek, wantlistFound) were destructured but never consumed by the JSX. Underscore-prefixing would silence the lint but leave 3 unnecessary DB round-trips per page load. Deletion both fixes lint and improves performance.
- **Refactored modal backdrop in edit-profile-modal vs. silencing the rule:** The current `<div role="button">` wrapping interactive content (close button, fields) is genuinely user-hostile (tab order is broken). Refactoring to a separate backdrop `<button>` resolves Biome AND fixes the a11y problem the rule was warning about.
- **Converted button-groups to `<fieldset>` in QualityDeclarationModal vs. silencing:** Biome was right — semantic `<fieldset>`/`<legend>` is the correct markup for a group of related buttons. The visual styling carries over with `border-0 p-0 m-0`.
- **Used `ReturnType<typeof Ratelimit.slidingWindow>` for the limiter type:** Upstash's `Algorithm<TContext>` is exported but `RegionContext` is internal. `ReturnType` extracts the concrete type without depending on the private symbol.
- **Did not fix the remaining 90 warnings:** Per scope discipline (deviation rule SCOPE BOUNDARY) — the plan's success criterion is "lint exit 0", not "zero warnings". Warnings are tracked in evidence/02a-lint-final.txt for future cleanup phases.
