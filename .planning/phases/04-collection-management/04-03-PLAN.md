    - Show per-item loading spinner while adding (set `isAdding = result.discogsId`)
    - On success: show toast via `sonner` ("Record added to collection"), close dialog, call `router.refresh()` to reload the collection grid
    - On error (duplicate): show toast error ("Record already in your collection")

        <div className="px-1">
          <Input placeholder="Search by title or artist..." value={query} onChange={...} className="font-mono" />
        </div>
        <div className="flex-1 overflow-y-auto min-h-0 mt-2">
          {isSearching && <loading spinner>}
          {results.map(r => <result row>)}
